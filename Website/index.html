Our challenge

First: design a neural network capable of analyzing star brightness curves and train it to differentiate between those caused by planet transits and other causes.

Second: put it to good use with an interactive site for people to use!

Website goal

We have made this website and implemented our AI in it as our submission for the 2025 NASA Space Apps global Hackathon, and as a simple way for people to learn about exoplanets. Why? Because it’s fun. We wish you all the very best of luck!
