Our Challenge

Design a neural network that distinguishes transit-shaped dimmings from other noise, then turn it into an interactive website.

Project Highlights

How our AI works

Upload a CSV brightness curve. The model analyzes patterns and returns the probability that the signal is a planet transit. An animation is generated to visualise the result.
