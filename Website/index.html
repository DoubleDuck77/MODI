Our Challenge

Firstly: Designing and training a neural network for stellar light curves analysis and differentiation between planetary transits and other events.

Secondly: Putting it to good use by the means of an interactive, highly accessible website for both professionals and amateurs to utilize!

Website Goal

We created this website and implemented our AI as our submission for the 2025 NASA Space Apps Global Hackathon, while also being a fun and original way for people to learn about exoplanets.
